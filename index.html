Privacy Policy for Twitch Faceoff Extension

Last Updated: November 4, 2024

1. Information We Collect

The Twitch Faceoff Extension collects the following information:

  • Your Twitch ID and username when you use the extension
  • Your participation status in lobbies
  • Bits transactions related to lobby entry
  • Channel-specific preferences and settings for broadcasters

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Manage lobby participation and access
  • Process challenge requests between broadcasters
  • Verify viewer eligibility for lobby entry
  • Track lobby participant counts
  • Enable Bits transactions

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored temporarily and securely on our servers. Participant data is cleared when new lobby settings are saved. We do not permanently store personal information beyond what is necessary for the extension's functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Twitch API for authentication and Bits transactions
  • Heroku for hosting our backend services

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of using the extension

6. Data Retention

User participation data is temporary and cleared when:

  • New lobby settings are saved
  • Challenges are accepted or declined
  • The extension is uninstalled

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us at crossvidapp@gmail.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the extension or Twitch's notification system.
